About us

Our company Tenerife North Cleaning Service is a Polish team offering personalized services in holiday and commercial cleaning, property management, and upholstery cleaning. We have extensive knowledge in maintaining cleanliness and the appealing look of your home. We care about ensuring our clients are satisfied with our services.

The idea to start the company was born in Iceland, where we gained years of experience in this field. We know that high quality and professionalism are expected by clients, and these are our top priorities.

We approach each client individually. Your satisfaction is our priority.

If you own an apartment, house, or office, we are a trusted and experienced company that will keep your property clean. We offer one-time or recurring cleaning services to keep your home always clean and fresh.

Prices are determined individually depending on the service requested. You can contact us via the form on our website, Facebook, or WhatsApp.

We work with experienced professionals who can quickly and effectively solve any issues in your rental property to ensure everything works 100%.

If you own a property in the north of Tenerife and would like to fully capitalize on it, we also offer management services for holiday apartments with a Vivienda Vacacional license.

We charge 15% of the rental income (after deducting other fees and costs). Cleaning, linen, towels, and maintenance costs are additional. We provide detailed invoices for services rendered to ensure full transparency.
